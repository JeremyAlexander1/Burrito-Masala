ABOUT OUR CART

Burrito Masala is a food cart located in downtown Portland, Oregon on the corner of SW 3rd and Alder. Burrito Masala specializes in cooking delicious southern Indian (Kerala) and Mexican food. Burrito Masala offers options to build burritos or bowls, and you can pick your ingredients.

Burrito Masala is created and operated by husband and wife team (James Beard award-winning chefs Prasad Varma and Ramona Varma-Hernandez). The Burrito Masala experience is unique between the ordering process of allowing customers to assemble their own meals, consuming diverse cultures foods, and the interactive service from award-winning chefs.
